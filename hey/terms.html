Orders

Orders are reviewed by NUVELLE HOME after payment so pickup, delivery, and white-glove timing can be confirmed.

Pickup and delivery are unavailable on weekends. Delivery fees are calculated by ZIP code when available. Some addresses or large-item orders may require store confirmation.

Clearance

Clearance items are final sale. Clearance pricing may reflect new inventory arriving, discontinued items, or packaging changes.

Returns and Damages

Customers should inspect items at pickup or delivery. Contact NUVELLE HOME promptly if an item arrives damaged or incorrect.

Warranty

Warranty coverage depends on the item and supplier. Contact customer care for product-specific support.
