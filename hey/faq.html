Got questions? We can help.

Ordering Shipping Payments Clearance
How can I place an order?

Add the pieces you want to cart, review your order, then continue to secure checkout. You can also ask Nuvelle Care about a product before ordering.

Can I change an order after checkout?

Contact Nuvelle Home as soon as possible. Changes depend on payment status, item availability, and whether pickup or delivery has already been scheduled.

What shipping options are available?

Pickup, ZIP-based delivery, and white-glove service may be available. Delivery is confirmed with the store before fulfillment.

Do you deliver on weekends?

No. Nuvelle Home is open Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM-5:00 PM, and closed on weekends.

How are payments handled?

Checkout is prepared for Stripe, so card details are handled through secure payment infrastructure rather than stored by the storefront.

Can I choose pickup at checkout?

Yes. Pickup can be selected during checkout, and Nuvelle Home will confirm timing.

Are clearance items final sale?

Yes. Clearance items are final sale and may be discounted because inventory is changing, the item is discontinued, or packaging has changed.

Can I ask about an item before buying?

Yes. Use the product page or live chat to ask Nuvelle Care about measurements, delivery, pickup, or availability.

Need further assistance?

Nuvelle Care can help with product questions, delivery timing, and checkout support.
