About Nuvelle

Comfort, proportion, and pieces that make a room feel considered.

NUVELLE HOME is a Miami-based furniture store for customers who want polished rooms without losing comfort. We help customers choose pieces that feel refined, practical, and ready for the way they actually live.

Our Point Of View

Furniture should make the room quieter, warmer, and easier to live in.

We look for silhouettes with presence, finishes that feel mature, and proportions that work in real homes. A sofa should invite people in. A dining table should make hosting feel natural. Storage should solve a problem without making the room feel heavy.

From our Miami base, Nuvelle Home supports customers with product questions, pickup, delivery planning, and white-glove options for larger pieces.
