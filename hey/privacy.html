Privacy policy.

NUVELLE HOME collects the information needed to process orders, answer customer care messages, arrange pickup or delivery, and send store updates when a customer subscribes.

Information We Use

Order details, contact information, delivery address, chat messages, and product questions may be stored in the store admin system so the Nuvelle team can provide support.

Payments

Payment information is handled by Stripe. NUVELLE HOME does not store card numbers on this website.

Customer Care

Messages sent through live chat may include the page or product the customer was viewing, so the store can answer with better context.

To request help with your information, contact nuvellehomedecor@gmail.com.
