Shipping

Pickup, delivery, and white-glove service.

Nuvelle Home confirms delivery details before fulfillment so furniture arrives with the right timing, handling, and contact information.

What delivery options are available?

Customers can choose pickup, ZIP-based delivery, or white-glove service when available. Final delivery options are reviewed during checkout.

Do you deliver outside Miami?

Delivery fees are calculated by ZIP code when available. Orders outside supported delivery zones may require a quote from Nuvelle Care.

Can I pick up my order?

Yes. Pickup is available by confirmation from the store. Weekend pickup is unavailable because the store is closed Saturday and Sunday.

What is white-glove service?

White-glove service is for orders that need careful handling and placement. The checkout flow can collect this request so the store can confirm availability and timing.

What if my order arrives damaged?

Contact Nuvelle Home immediately with photos and your order details. The team will review the issue and guide the next step.
